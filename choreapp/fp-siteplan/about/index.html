Why Chore Champion?

The first benefit of our app is that it helps reduce conflict between parents and children. The app serves as the middleman between the parent and child so that parents don't have to personally confront the child about chores. This is done because instead of the child getting all the chores from their parents, they get their chores from a list in the app. The kids can easily see all the chores they have to do without having to be told by their parents. The app also motivates the child to do the chores instead of having to be from their parents. Every time that the child completes a chore, they are rewarded with points. These points can be redeemed for an allowance, more screen time, or a fun event such as laser tag. These fun prizes would motivate the child to do the chores so that the parent doesn't have to be the bad guy and make them do their chores. By not being the bad guy every day, lots of conflicts will be avoided.


Another benefit to the app is that it motivates kids to do the chores around the house that never get done. In a lot of houses, there are chores that need to be done, but never do because nobody wants to actually do them. If these chores that never get done are put into the app, and there is an actual reward to do them, then the children will be far more likely to do them. This would be really good for the parents because then all the things that they don’t have time to do will finally be done. This is also really good for the kids because, without the app, the kids would probably have to do it anyway, but now they are getting rewards.


The last benefit of the app is that it makes chores fun for kids. For young kids, most of their life is fun and play. Even school at a young age is mostly fun. One of the only things that aren't fun is their chores, so they hate them. Chores can cause children to tantrum and put them in a bad mood. Since Chore Champion is so effective in solving this issue it will push kids and parents to continue using the app.
