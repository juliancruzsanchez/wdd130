Purpose

Kids hate doing their chores, often leaving parents frustrated with their children.
So I've decided to make an app that motivates kids to do their chores with experience based rewards from local businesses.

Audience

Two example people who would be in my target market are:

  • Timmy
  • 8 years old
  • Hates doing his chores
  • Plays games on his tablet
  • Lauren
  • $80,000 Household income
  • Has two kids, 8 year old boy and 5 year old girl
  • Fights with her children about chores
