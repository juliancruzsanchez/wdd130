Purpose

Problem getting your kids to do chores?
You're not alone, 72% of parents do chores themselves to avoid conflict.
I've decided to make an app that emotivates kids to do their chores with experience based rewards from local businesses.

Benefits

Why Us?

Competitive Advantage

Our app advertises discounts to parents, which can be given to kids as rewards for completing chores, helping parents save money. Since we partner with local businesses, parents can have fun with their child without having to go out of their way, and support local businesses in the process.

Another aspect of the app is its unique partnerships with other businesses, it is the only app that rewards kids with products and experiences from businesses for completing their chores .

Local businesses can benefit from our app because it provides them with a method to reach their target market more effectively. We not only get kids and parents to use their business, but the businesses also get to be associated with the reward. This can help promote their brand while driving sales and boosting revenue.
