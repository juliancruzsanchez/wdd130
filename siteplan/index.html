Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote and inform returning and prospecting customers of our services, mission, purpose and to allow them to browse and purchase our tours. We also provide support to customers on this site.

Audience

This website is targeted at prospective and returning customers of Dry Oar Rafting. This target market consists of outdoorsy middle-class individuals. Most likely adults or families so ages 17-55 is the expected age range.
